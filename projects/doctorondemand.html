Doctor-on-Demand

Doctor on Demand enables us to speak with experienced and licensed specialist doctors instantly at any time of day through video conferencing. Me and my teammate developed this app in a 24 hour offline hackathon Hack NSUT and implemented Agora in it. Designed front-end in NextJs,back-end by NodeJs and MongoDB to store users and doctors profile in database.
